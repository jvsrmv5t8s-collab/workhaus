## 3. Workspace Solutions

### 3.1 Hot Desks
- **Best for:** Freelancers, remote workers, early-stage startups
- **Pricing:** Starting from $295–$350/month (varies by location)
- **Terms:** Month-to-month or term agreements
- **Typical team size:** 1–8 people
- **IT Support:** Standard IT support
- **Meeting room credits:** Based on location
- **What's included:**
  - Access to a wide variety of eclectic workstations and lounge seating
  - Access to meeting rooms, phone booths, breakout spaces, and kitchens
  - Work out of multiple collaborative spaces within the facility
  - No assigned seat or lockable storage
  - Clients may visit but cannot work from the space after the meeting
- **Memberships are non-transferable** — each person must have their own membership

### 3.2 Dedicated Desks
- **Best for:** Freelancers, remote workers, small teams, growing startups
- **Pricing:** Starting from $395–$495/month (varies by location)
- **Terms:** Month-to-month or term agreements
- **Typical team size:** 1–16 people
- **IT Support:** Standard IT support
- **Meeting room credits:** Based on location
- **What's included:**
  - Your own permanently assigned seat in a vibrant open-concept area
  - Lockable pedestal filing cabinet for personal belongings and sensitive materials
  - Access to all shared facilities (lounges, kitchens, breakout areas, meeting rooms, phone rooms)
  - Can leave personal items and equipment at the desk overnight
  - Clients may visit but cannot work from the space after the meeting
- **Memberships are non-transferable**
- Teams of more than 4 can explore FlexDesk booking system options

### 3.3 Private Offices
- **Best for:** Growing startups, small-to-medium sized businesses
- **Pricing:** Starting from $1,300/month
- **Terms:** Month-to-month or term agreements
- **Typical team size:** 2–30 people
- **IT Support:** Advanced IT support available for large spaces
- **Meeting room credits:** Differs by office size
- **What's included:**
  - Turnkey, fully furnished with desks, task chairs, and lockable pedestal filing cabinets
  - Private, lockable office space
  - Move-in ready — "plug and play"
  - Option to bring your own furniture (facilities team will handle swapping)
  - Multiple collaborative spaces within the facility
  - Secure space with option for advanced IT support (conditions apply)
  - Clients may visit
- **Right of First Refusal** offered on larger spaces as teams grow
- Fixed price per office includes a designated number of memberships

### 3.4 Corporate Packages
- **Best for:** Large corporates, enterprises, teams with a hybrid workforce model
- **Pricing:** From $2,500/month (some locations from $2,000/month)
- **Terms:** Month-to-month or term agreements
- **Typical team size:** 10+ people
- **IT Support:** Advanced support for private spaces
- **Meeting room credits:** Customized for each package
- **What's included:**
  - Bespoke, custom-built office space packages
  - Free desk booking software (FlexDesk) for scheduling in-office staff rotations
  - Interactive floor plan for team to book desks daily, weekly, or monthly
  - Full suite of services and amenities
  - Flexible terms
  - Can bring own furniture and equipment
- **Right of First Refusal** offered on larger spaces as teams grow

### 3.5 Virtual Offices
- **Best for:** Businesses needing a professional address without a physical presence
- **Pricing:** Starting at $75/month
- **Terms:** Month-to-month
- **Available at select locations:** Toronto (181 University Ave., 215 Spadina Ave., 2 Sheppard Ave. E.), Kitchener (290 King St. E.), Calgary (324 8th Ave. SW)
- **What's included:**
  - Professional business address at a premium Canadian location
  - Mail handling and secure storage until pickup
  - Mail pickup notifications from on-site team
  - 10% discount on meeting room bookings at select locations
  - Use of address on corporate collateral
- **Important limitations:**
  - Letter mail only (under 1 lb); special arrangements for packages must be discussed
  - Virtual members cannot work out of the office space (upgrade to Hot Desk for that)
  - One membership = one company, one location
- **Available Add-Ons:**
  - Automated Receptionist — Starting at $60/month (local number, automated greetings, call forwarding)
  - Live Reception — Starting at $295/month (professional receptionists to answer calls and take messages)
  - Meeting Room Hours — Starting at $50/month (discounted hourly packages)

### 3.6 Meeting Rooms
- **Best for:** Client meetings, team sessions, presentations, brainstorming
- **Pricing:**
  - Small rooms (1–6 people): Starting at $65/hour
  - Large rooms (8–14 people): Starting at $85–$95/hour (varies by location)
- **Available in:** Toronto, Kitchener-Waterloo, Calgary
- **What's included:**
  - Blazing fast WiFi
  - Presentation screens
  - Whiteboards and flipcharts
  - Dedicated on-site team for support
  - Hourly and day bookings
  - Catering options available
- **Important policies:**
  - Bookings are only guaranteed for the agreed time. Book extra time if you anticipate running over.
  - After the meeting, non-members cannot continue working in the space (day passes available for $35/person)
  - Can bring own AV equipment (confirm compatibility with IT team first)
  - Book by filling out the contact form; a team member will follow up

## 6. Membership & Policies — FAQs

### Pricing & Fees
- **No hidden fees.** Everything is included in one transparent monthly fee.
- The only additional costs are virtual services (phone lines, live/digital answering services), billed separately.

### Contract Terms
- **Month-to-month options** are available on all workspace solutions.
- Month-to-month cancellations require **30 days' notice**, handled on a calendar month basis.
- Fixed-term agreements are also available.

### Growth & Scaling
- Workhaus actively communicates with members to help plan for growth.
- **Right of First Refusal** is typically offered on larger spaces as teams expand.
- Teams can scale up or down at short notice.

### Membership Sharing
- Memberships are **non-transferable**. Each person must have their own membership.
- Teams with 4+ members in a private office or dedicated desk plan can explore **FlexDesk** scheduling options.

### Booking Meeting Rooms
- Members receive login credentials for the **Workhaus mobile and web app**.
- From the app: select a meeting room, choose date and duration, and confirm.
- The app also shows partner discounts, upcoming events, and community updates.

### Bringing Clients
- Members across all plans can bring clients in for meetings.
- Clients are welcome to stay for the meeting but **cannot continue working** from the space afterward.

### Leaving Belongings
- **Hot Desk members:** Cannot leave belongings at the space (no assigned seat or storage).
- **Dedicated Desk members:** Can leave belongings on the desk and in a lockable pedestal filing cabinet.

### Day Passes
- Day passes are available for **$35 per person** for non-members who want to work from the space (e.g., after a meeting).

### Upgrading
- Contact your Community Lead to explore other workspace options; the team will help find the right fit.
